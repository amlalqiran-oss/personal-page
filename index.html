FAQ

Common Questions

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in modern frontend technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), React, and various CSS frameworks like Tailwind CSS. I also work with build tools, version control systems, and modern development workflows.

How long does it take to build a website?

The timeline varies based on project complexity. A simple landing page might take 1-2 weeks, while a more complex web application could take 4-8 weeks. I always provide a detailed timeline estimate after understanding your requirements.

How do you stay updated with new web trends?

I follow industry blogs, participate in developer communities, attend webinars, and regularly experiment with new technologies in side projects. Continuous learning is essential in the ever-evolving web development landscape.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes! I offer post-launch support packages to ensure your website runs smoothly. This includes bug fixes, minor updates, and performance monitoring. We can discuss the support terms based on your needs.
